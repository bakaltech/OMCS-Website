Our Journey

2000

Foundation

OMCS begins its mission to support immigrant families and vulnerable communities in Ottawa.

2008

Community Programs

Launch of youth mentorship and settlement programs, reaching hundreds of families.

2015

Mental Health Expansion

Expanded counselling and mental wellness services, adding trauma‑informed care.

2020

Virtual Services

Rapidly adapted to offer online counselling and workshops during the pandemic.

2023

Growing Impact

Thousands of families supported across Ontario; new partnership with local health networks.

2025

25th Anniversary

Celebrating 25 years of service with expanded programs and new community centre.

Counselling sessions 1,500+
