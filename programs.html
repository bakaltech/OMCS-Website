Our Programs

Empowering individuals and families through culturally‑responsive services, education, and community support.

At OMCS, we design programs that meet the unique needs of Ottawa's Muslim community. From youth mentorship to senior support, every initiative is rooted in compassion, cultural understanding, and a commitment to excellence. Explore our offerings below and find the support you need.

How We Help

Culturally Sensitive

Holistic Care

Supporting mind, body, and spirit together.

Program FAQs

Who can participate in your programs?

+

Most programs are open to all, with some designed specifically for youth, families, or seniors. Check individual program details for eligibility.

Are there any costs?

+

Many programs are offered at low or no cost. For some workshops, a small fee may apply. Contact us for details.

How do I register for a program?

+

You can register by clicking "Learn more" on any program card and using the registration link, or by contacting us directly.
