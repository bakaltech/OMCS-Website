Frequently Asked Questions

What can I expect in my first session?

+

Your first session is an opportunity to get to know your therapist and share what brings you to counselling. We'll discuss your goals, any concerns, and how we can best support you.

Is counselling confidential?

+

Absolutely. All sessions are confidential within the limits of the law (e.g., risk of harm). We prioritise your privacy and safety.

Do you accept insurance?

+

We provide receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider. Please check with your plan for coverage details.

How long are sessions?

+

Typical sessions are 50 minutes, but we can discuss your needs and adjust accordingly.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

+

Yes, all therapists offer online sessions via secure video. You can choose online or in‑person when booking.

How do I choose the right therapist?

+

You can read our therapists' profiles above. Many offer a free 15‑minute consultation to help you decide who feels right for you.

What is the cost per session?

+

Our fees are competitive and we offer a sliding scale based on financial need. Please contact us for more information.

🔒 Your privacy is our priority

All sessions are confidential. We adhere to strict professional ethics and privacy laws. You can speak freely.
