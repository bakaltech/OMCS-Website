Volunteer With Us

Join a community of compassionate individuals dedicated to making a difference in Ottawa's Muslim community.

Volunteers are the heart of OMCS. Whether you're packing food hampers, mentoring youth, or helping at events, your time and skills create lasting impact. We welcome volunteers of all backgrounds and abilities – training is provided, and flexible schedules are available.
