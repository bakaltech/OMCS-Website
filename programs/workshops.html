About Workshops

Our workshops are designed to equip individuals and families with practical skills, knowledge, and tools for everyday challenges. Topics are chosen based on community needs and delivered by experienced facilitators.

Sessions are interactive, culturally sensitive, and offered both online and in‑person.

Workshop FAQs

Are workshops free?

Most workshops are offered at no cost. Some specialized sessions may have a small fee to cover materials.

Do I need to register in advance?

Yes, registration is required to secure your spot and receive the joining link or location details.

Can I suggest a workshop topic?

Absolutely! We welcome community input. Contact us with your ideas.
