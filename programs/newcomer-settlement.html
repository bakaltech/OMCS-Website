Newcomer Settlement

Guidance and support for newcomers to integrate and thrive in Ottawa.

About Settlement Services

Our settlement program helps newcomers with housing, employment, language classes, and cultural orientation. We offer one‑on‑one support and group workshops.

We are here to help you navigate systems, access resources, and build a new life with dignity and confidence.

Quick Facts

  • ✓ Languages: Arabic, Dari, Pashto, Urdu, English, French
  • ✓ Assistance with government forms
  • ✓ Employment & housing referrals
  • ✓ Free of charge
  • ✓ No status documentation required

Settlement FAQs

Is there a cost?

All settlement services are completely free.

Do I need immigration status to access services?

We help everyone regardless of status. Your information is kept confidential.

Can you help with translation?

Yes, we offer translation and interpretation assistance in multiple languages.
