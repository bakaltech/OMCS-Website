About Our Events

OMCS hosts events throughout the year to bring the community together – from iftars during Ramadan to Eid celebrations, health fairs, and networking nights. All events are open to everyone.

These gatherings are a chance to connect, share experiences, and strengthen the fabric of our community.

Event FAQs

Are events free?

Most events are free. Some special gatherings may have a nominal fee to cover costs.

Can I volunteer at events?

Absolutely! We always welcome volunteers. Visit our Get Involved page for more information.

Are events family-friendly?

Yes, most events are designed for all ages. Check the event description for details.
