About Senior Support

Our senior support program offers weekly social gatherings, gentle exercise classes, wellness check‑ins, and assistance with navigating services.

We aim to reduce isolation and help seniors maintain independence, dignity, and connection.

Quick Facts

  • ✓ Gentle exercise classes
  • ✓ Wellness check‑ins by phone
  • ✓ Free transportation available

Senior FAQs

Do I need to be a certain age?

Our programs are designed for seniors 55 and older.

Is transportation really free?

Yes, we offer free pickup within Ottawa for those who need it. Just let us know when you register.

Can I volunteer to help with senior programs?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers of all ages. Visit our Get Involved page for more information.
