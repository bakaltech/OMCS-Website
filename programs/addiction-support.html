Addiction Support

Counselling and resources for individuals and families affected by addiction.

About the Program

We provide confidential, non‑judgmental support for those struggling with addiction and their loved ones. Our culturally sensitive approach respects your background and values.

Services include one‑on‑one counselling, support groups (for individuals and families), and referrals to specialized programs when needed.

Quick Facts

  • ✓ One‑on‑one counselling
  • ✓ Support groups (individuals & families)
  • ✓ Confidential & non‑judgmental
  • ✓ Culturally sensitive care
  • ✓ Sliding scale available

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are confidential within the limits of the law. We prioritize your privacy.

Do you treat all types of addiction?

Yes, we support individuals facing any form of addiction, including substances, gambling, and behavioural addictions.

Can family members attend sessions?

Yes. We offer family support sessions and encourage loved ones to be part of the healing process.
