Family FAQs

What topics do parenting workshops cover?

Topics include positive discipline, communication with teens, understanding child development, and managing stress as a parent.

Is family counselling confidential?

Yes, all counselling sessions are confidential. We adhere to strict professional ethics.

What ages are suitable for play therapy?

Play therapy is typically for children ages 3–10, but we adapt approaches for all ages.
