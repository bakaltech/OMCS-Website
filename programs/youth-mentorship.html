Youth Mentorship

One‑on‑one mentoring for teens to build confidence, life skills, and a sense of belonging.

About the Program

Our Youth Mentorship program connects teens (ages 13–18) with caring adult mentors who provide guidance, encouragement, and a positive role model. Mentors and mentees meet weekly for one hour over a 12‑week period.

Activities are tailored to each teen's interests and goals – from academic support and career exploration to building social skills and self‑confidence.

Quick Facts

  • ✓ Duration: 12 weeks
  • ✓ Weekly sessions: 1 hour
  • ✓ Eligibility: Teens 13–18
  • ✓ Cost: Free
  • ✓ Next cohort: Starts March 15

Frequently Asked Questions

How are mentors screened?

All mentors undergo a criminal background check, reference checks, and an interview. They also receive training in youth engagement and safety.

Can parents be involved?

Absolutely. We keep parents informed and encourage them to be part of the journey. Mentors and parents have regular check‑ins.

What if we need to cancel a session?

No problem – just let us know in advance. Sessions can be rescheduled easily.
